How to get Ottoman Lira ($OTLR)

The procedure below is a summary on how to buy Ottoman Lira ($OTLR), for a more detailed tutorial click the link here

Step 1

Create a MetaMask Wallet

$OTLR token is available on the Polygon blockchain. MetaMask is a third party ERC20 browser wallet, and the very best at that! On Google Chrome, visit metamask.io to download the extension and set up a wallet. On mobile? Get MetaMask's app for iPhone or Android.

Step 2

Add Polygon network on MetaMask

You can add polygon network to MetaMask using the official guide from the Polygon team which you can access from the link here

Step 3

Send $MATIC to MetaMask Wallet

You can buy $MATIC Token from 25+ Centralised as well as Decentralised exchanges. The list of which is given here

Step 4

Visit Uniswap

Uniswap is the world's best Decentralised exchanges, and as of now you can buy $OTLR only through Uniswap. You can vist Uniswap from the link here

Step 5

Switch to polygon network on uniswap

Make sure to switch to polygon network before swaping $OTLR. You can switch to polygon by choosing polygon from the drop-down menu on Uniswap

Step 6

Swap $MATIC for $OTLR

You can swap $MATIC for $OTLR after connecting your wallet on Uniswap. After clicking on swap you'll be prompted with the confirmation prompt by MetaMask, click approve and you'll get $OTLR in your MetaMask Wallet in no time.

Wiki

Ottoman Lira ($OTLR)

The Ottoman lira (sign: ₤) was the currency of Ottoman Empire between 1844 and 29 October 1923 when it was replaced by the Turkish lira. To honour the historic currency the Lira Foundation has created the digitalised version of the historic currency named after it, Ottoman Lira ($OTLR) using smart contracts on ERC20 Standards
